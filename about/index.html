HOW WAS 1994?

The Shawshank Redemption and Lion King were filmed.

Yanni and Cohen released their most popular live albums.

The first cable connection to Internet in China was built.

Before all events took place, by the river lands where Emperor Shun trained people how to plow and where the earliest Chinese celadon was found,

There I was born.
